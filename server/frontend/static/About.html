About Us

Welcome to Best Cars Dealership, home to the best cars in North America. We specialize in the sale of domestic and imported cars at reasonable prices, ensuring quality and value for every customer.

Led by our fearless CEO Luffy, sharp-minded CFO Zoro, and suave COO Sanji, we bring adventure, precision, and top-tier service to every deal. Whether you're chasing speed, luxury, or reliability, our crew is here to help you find your road and chase your adventure. 🚗🔥

Lucas D. Gear

Chief Executive Officer

Lucas "Luffy" D. Gear, CEO of *Best Cars Dealership*, grew up in a family of mechanics but always dreamed bigger—he wanted to build a dealership where *anyone* could find their perfect ride. At 17, he set out on an adventure, learning the trade, outsmarting shady dealers, and assembling a loyal crew of expert mechanics and salespeople. With his fearless energy, wild promotions (like free road trips with every car purchase), and signature red vest and straw hat, Lucas turned *Best Cars Dealership* into more than just a business—it became a place where people *chase their adventure*. His motto? *"Find your road, chase your adventure."* 🚗💨

Roronoa Zoro

Chief Financial Officer

Roronoa "Zoro" Steele, the no-nonsense CFO of *Best Cars Dealership*, treats finances like a high-speed race—precise, strategic, and never off course. A former street racer turned financial mastermind, he ensures the dealership runs efficiently, cutting waste while maximizing value for customers. Despite his talent with numbers, he has a terrible sense of direction and often gets lost in the office. Known for his sharp negotiation skills and even sharper instincts, Zoro believes every dollar should be spent wisely, just like every mile should be driven with purpose. His motto? *“Money, like horsepower, is useless if you don’t know how to control it.”* 💰⚔️🚗

Sanji

Chief Operating Officer

Sanji "Vins" Leclerc, the smooth-talking COO of *Best Cars Dealership*, runs operations with the precision of a master chef—every detail must be perfect.
